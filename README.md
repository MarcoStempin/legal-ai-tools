# Legal AI Tools

A collection of AI-powered tools for in-house legal teams, built with Claude (Anthropic).

Built by a senior in-house lawyer and M&A attorney exploring how AI can reduce 
turnaround times and increase quality in everyday legal work.

## About

This repository documents a hands-on learning journey at the intersection of 
corporate law and AI – translating real legal workflows into practical tools.

Tools will be added as they are developed.
